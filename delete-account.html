Deleting an Account

Last updated October 3rd, 2025

Users can delete their account in the Divvy app via going to the profile tab, clicking the gear icon in the top right corner to open User Settings, and clicking the "Delete my account" button. Divvy will immediately delete your account and the data associated with it. Any activity in groups you participated in will show "Unknown User" in place of your account.
