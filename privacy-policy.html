Privacy Policy

Last updated September 11th, 2025

Introduction

Divvy respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

Information We Collect

When you use our App, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your phone number, display name, and username.
  • Verification Data: Phone number verification codes to confirm your identity.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including actions performed, features accessed, and interactions within the App.
  • Camera Access: If you choose to use the receipt scanning feature, we access your device’s camera and/or photo library. These images are processed for the intended purpose and are not stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To provide and improve the App’s functionality and services.
  • To ensure data security, detect fraud, and prevent unauthorized access.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account and service updates.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our App, such as cloud hosting on AWS.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect rights, safety, or security.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely using encryption in Amazon Web Services (AWS).
  • We use authentication mechanisms and session tokens to protect access to your account.
  • We retain your data only as long as necessary for providing our services, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

User Rights

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time directly within the App. This will remove your personal data from our active systems.
  • Access & Correction: You may access or update your account information within the App.
  • Legal Rights: If applicable, you may have additional rights under specific laws (such as GDPR for EU users or CCPA for California residents). Please contact us for more information regarding these rights.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13 [or 16, if applicable under your jurisdiction]. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child without proper consent, we will delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, we will notify users within the App as well as via the website. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy will reflect the latest version.
